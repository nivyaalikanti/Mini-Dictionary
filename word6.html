Ephemeral

Definition

Lasting for a very short time.

Use Cases

  • The beauty of a sunset is often described as ephemeral, fading quickly into night.
  • Many insects, like mayflies, have an ephemeral adult life, lasting only a day or two.
  • Fame can be an ephemeral thing; one day you're celebrated, the next forgotten.
  • The joy of winning was ephemeral, soon overshadowed by the next challenge.
  • Childhood, in retrospect, seems incredibly ephemeral to most adults.
Synonyms
Fleeting Transient Short-lived
Momentary Brief Temporary
Antonyms
Permanent Enduring Everlasting
Eternal Perpetual Long-lasting
