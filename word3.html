Obambulate

Definition

To walk about or wander aimlessly without a fixed direction or purpose.

Use Cases

  • She loved to obambulate through the city streets, discovering hidden cafes and murals.
  • After dinner, he would obambulate in the park, lost in his thoughts.
  • The tourists chose to obambulate through the village rather than follow a strict itinerary.
  • He often obambulated through the museum, letting the exhibits guide his path.
  • Sometimes, the best ideas come when you obambulate with no destination in mind.
Synonyms
Wander Stroll Meander
Roam Saunter Drift
Antonyms
March Stride Rush
