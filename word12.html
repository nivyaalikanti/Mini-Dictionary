Gleam

Definition

A small or brief flash or shine of light; also used to describe a faint indication of something positive like hope or joy.

Use Cases

  • A gleam of sunlight broke through the clouds.
  • He had a mischievous gleam in his eyes.
  • The ring had a faint golden gleam under the lamp.
  • A gleam of hope lit up her face when she heard the news.
  • The glass gleamed after being cleaned thoroughly.
Synonyms
Shine Glow Twinkle
Flash Glimmer Sparkle
Antonyms
Dullness Darkness Gloom
Shadow Fade Dimness
