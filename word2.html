Concinnity

Definition

Refers to the skillful and harmonious arrangement of parts, creating a pleasing or effective whole.

Use Cases

  • A song where the music and lyrics flow perfectly together demonstrates concinnity.
  • A beautifully designed building where every part looks right and works together has concinnity.
  • A well-written story with all the plot points and characters fitting together neatly shows concinnity.
  • A speech that is smooth, logical, and flows easily from one idea to the next exhibits concinnity.
  • A painter creating a harmonious blend of colors and forms in their work achieves concinnity.
Synonyms
Harmony Balance Symmetry
Unity Coherence Elegance
Antonyms
Disorder Disharmony Imbalance
Incoherence Disproportion Disunity
