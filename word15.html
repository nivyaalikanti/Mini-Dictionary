Wane

Definition

To slowly decrease or fade away in strength, size, or importance.

Use Cases

  • The moon began to wane after reaching its full phase.
  • His excitement started to wane as the day went on.
  • Public interest in the topic began to wane over time.
  • Her energy waned after hours of hiking.
  • Fame may shine brightly at first, but it can quickly wane.
Synonyms
Fade Dim Diminish
Decline Shrink Subside
Antonyms
Grow Increase Rise
Expand Strengthen Escalate
