Reverie

Definition

A dreamy state of pleasant thoughts; daydreaming.

Use Cases

  • She drifted into a reverie as the music played softly.
  • The quiet afternoon inspired a sweet reverie.
  • His reverie was broken by the sudden knock at the door.
  • Lost in reverie, he smiled at old memories.
  • She wrote poems during moments of deep reverie.
Synonyms
Dream Fantasy Daydream
Trance Imagination Musing
Antonyms
Reality Awareness Focus
Concentration Mindfulness Attention
