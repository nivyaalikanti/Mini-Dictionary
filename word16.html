Glimpse

Definition

A short, quick view or momentary experience of something or someone.

Use Cases

  • I caught a glimpse of her smile before she turned away.
  • Through the fog, we had a glimpse of the distant mountains.
  • The diary offered a glimpse into his private world.
  • The bird vanished after a quick glimpse from behind the trees.
  • She got a glimpse of the future in her dream.
Synonyms
Peek Flash Glance
Look Sight View
Antonyms
Stare Gaze Inspection
Examination Study Scrutiny
