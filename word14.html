Placid

Definition

Not easily upset or excited; calm and peaceful.

Use Cases

  • The lake was placid in the morning light, reflecting the trees perfectly.
  • Despite the chaos around him, he remained remarkably placid.
  • She found a placid spot by the river where she could read in peace.
  • His placid demeanor made him an excellent mediator in disputes.
  • The cat lay in a placid slumber on the warm windowsill.
Synonyms
Calm Serene Tranquil
Peaceful Untroubled Composed
Antonyms
Agitated Turbulent Excited
Stormy Anxious Disturbed
