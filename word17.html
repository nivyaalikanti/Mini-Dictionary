Stillness

Definition

The quality of being quiet, calm, or without movement.

Use Cases

  • The stillness of the early morning was peaceful.
  • She meditated in complete stillness for an hour.
  • The forest was filled with a magical stillness after the snowfall.
  • Stillness before the storm often feels more tense than the storm itself.
  • He found comfort in the stillness of solitude.
Synonyms
Calm Quiet Peace
Silence Serenity Tranquility
Antonyms
Noise Chaos Movement
Commotion Rush Stir
