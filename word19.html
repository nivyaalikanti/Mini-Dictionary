Meager

Definition

Lacking in quantity or quality; very small or inadequate.

Use Cases

  • The refugees survived on a meager diet of rice and beans.
  • He earned a meager salary that barely covered his basic expenses.
  • Despite their meager resources, they managed to put on a wonderful play.
  • The evidence presented was quite meager and unconvincing.
  • A meager applause followed the uninspiring performance.
Synonyms
Scanty Sparse Paltry
Insufficient Limited Dwarfed
Antonyms
Abundant Plentiful Generous
Ample Substantial Copious
