Gimcracks

Definition

Showy objects of little use or value; trivial and cheaply ornamental items.

Use Cases

  • The souvenir shop was filled with all sorts of colorful gimcracks and trinkets.
  • She preferred functional items over decorative gimcracks that just collected dust.
  • His collection of old car parts and random mechanical gimcracks filled the garage.
  • The fair booth offered a variety of cheap gimcracks as prizes for the games.
  • Despite their showy appearance, most of the household's gimcracks were easily broken.
