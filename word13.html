Kindle

Definition

To light a fire or ignite an emotion, idea, or interest.

Use Cases

  • The campers used dry twigs to kindle a fire.
  • Her words kindled hope in their hearts.
  • A book can kindle curiosity in young minds.
  • They tried to kindle love where none had grown before.
  • The speech kindled a desire for change in the crowd.
Synonyms
Ignite Spark Inspire
Awaken Fuel Start
Antonyms
Extinguish Quench Suppress
Put out Cool Stifle
