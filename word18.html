Halcyon

Definition

Describing a period of time that was calm, happy, and peaceful.

Use Cases

  • They often reminisced about the halcyon days of their childhood.
  • The lake was still, reflecting the halcyon sky above.
  • It was a halcyon summer, full of warmth and laughter.
  • She longed for the halcyon peace of simpler times.
  • The village had a halcyon charm, untouched by modern life.
Synonyms
Peaceful Calm Serene
Tranquil Happy Golden
Antonyms
Chaotic Stormy Restless
Disturbed Stressful Unsettled
