Ubiquitous

Definition

Present, appearing, or found everywhere.

Use Cases

  • In today's world, smartphones have become truly ubiquitous.
  • Coffee shops seem to be ubiquitous in any major city.
  • The fear of surveillance is becoming increasingly ubiquitous in modern society.
  • Plastic waste is a ubiquitous problem, found in every corner of the globe.
  • Online advertisements are ubiquitous across the internet.
Synonyms
Omnipresent Pervasive Widespread
Everywhere Universal Common
Antonyms
Rare Scarce Limited
Uncommon Infrequent Localized
